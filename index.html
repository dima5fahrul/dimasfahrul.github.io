The Town Square

The town square of Sidoarjo is located in the center of Sidoarjo, East Java. It is located on the left side of the Malang-Surabaya highway, precisely on Ahmad Yani street, Sidoarjo city. As a marker in this square there is a monument in the form of a monument, decorated with statues of shrimp and milkfish. The monument which was inaugurated by the Regent of Sidoarjo, H. Saiful Ilah, SH., M.Hum. This monument is named Jayandaru Monument.

The layout of the Sidoarjo square is in the form of a garden, combined with sports facilities and various children's play facilities. So it is very suitable to be a tourist destination that can satisfy all family members. This square area is usually crowded on weekends, especially on Sundays. Where the protocol road around the square is the location for car free days, pampering the people of Sidoarjo to exercise without being disturbed by traffic.

Lusi Island

Lusi Island is a new island resulting from Sidoarjo mudflow deposits. The name Lusi is an acronym for 'Mudpur Sidoarjo. 'The unique name was given directly by the Minister of Fisheries and Marine Affairs at the time, namely Susi Pudjiastuti.

Lusi Island is better known as Sarinah Island by local residents. The area of the island is 93.4 hectares. The location is right in the middle of the Porong River, the border between Pasuruan and Sidoarjo, which is 25 kilometers from the land of Tlocor, Kedungpandan Jabon Village.

The site, which is now a tourist location, was formed as a result of the sedimentation process of the mud flow. The results of the mud sedimentation were then tried to be planted with mangroves. As a result, mangroves can grow well on the Sidoarjo silt island.

Sun City

Sun City Waterpark is the largest water recreation vehicle in Sidoarjo. Sun City Waterpark is in the Suncity Complex which is on Jl. Hero no. 1 Sidoarjo. From the outside, it looks small. The reason is, the gate is only about 10 meters with Egyptian-themed ornaments. Next to him, all that could be seen was a wall surrounded by trees. However, once inside, the water rides provided are quite extensive. Enough to accommodate about 7 thousand visitors.

Sun City Waterpark was built in 2004 with the aim of meeting the needs of the general public and the Sidoarjo community, especially for swimming pool recreation facilities in the city of Sidoarjo. Inside there are various forms of facilities, from stage entertainment, water games, dragon sliding, water boom. Sun City Waterpark is also equipped with Life Guard and Medical Center services to anticipate things that are not desirable.

Mpu Tantular State Museum

Mpu Tantular State Museum is a state museum located in Buduran district, Sidoarjo, East Java. Initially, the museum was named the Stedelijk Historisch Museum Soerabaia, founded by Godfried von Faber in 1933 and inaugurated on July 25, 1937. Currently, the museum is managed by the Technical Implementation Unit of the Ministry of Culture and Tourism.

The forerunner to the establishment of the Mpu Tantular State Museum was the establishment of the cultural institution Stedelijk Historisch Museum Soerabaia by Godfried Hariowald von Faber, a Surabaya resident of German nationality, in 1933, which was later inaugurated on July 25, 1937. The effort to expand the museum was carried out with the acquisition of a new building on Jalan Simpang (now Jalan Pemuda 3 Surabaya) and financed by funds collected from the community. The layout of this museum has a collection room, library, office space, auditorium. For the improvement of the museum he led, Von Faber held many international relations. But before his goal was achieved, Von Faber died on September 30, 1955.
